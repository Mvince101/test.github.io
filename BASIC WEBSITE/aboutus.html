Historical population

The population of Del Remedio grew from 9,020 in 1990 to 17,871 in 2020, an increase of 8,851 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 2.59%, or an increase of 2,042 people, from the previous population of 15,829 in 2015.
